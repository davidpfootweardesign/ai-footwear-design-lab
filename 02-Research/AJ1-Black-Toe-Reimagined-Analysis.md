# Air Jordan 1 High OG Black Toe Reimagined (2025)

## Research Notes – Upper Construction & Leather Panel Analysis

### Observations

* The upper is constructed from approximately **13 individual leather panels**.
* Nearly every panel overlaps another panel rather than simply meeting edge-to-edge.
* The Nike Swoosh is a separate stitched leather overlay instead of a printed graphic.

### Design Hypotheses

#### Multi-Panel Construction

The use of multiple leather panels appears to serve several purposes:

* Allows the upper to conform more naturally to the shape and movement of the foot.
* Creates controlled flex points so the shoe can move with the wearer while maintaining its overall structure.
* Reinforces high-stress areas by strategically layering material where additional strength is needed.
* Makes it easier to manufacture complex three-dimensional shapes than would be possible with a single large piece of leather.
* Supports Nike's iconic color blocking by allowing different colored leather panels to be assembled into the finished design.

#### Overlapping Panels

The overlapping construction likely provides several advantages:

* Reinforces areas subjected to repeated loading, especially around the lace eyelets.
* Distributes forces across multiple layers instead of concentrating stress in a single piece of leather.
* Improves structural integrity and helps the shoe retain its shape over time.
* Provides a cleaner, more durable construction than simply joining two panels edge-to-edge.
* Reduces the likelihood of gaps that could allow water or debris to enter through seams.

### Material Selection: Leather

Leather appears to have been selected because it offers an effective balance of:

* Abrasion resistance
* Tear strength
* Shape retention
* Long-term durability
* The ability to gradually conform to the wearer's foot

Compared with canvas or twill, leather generally provides greater durability and better resistance to wind and moisture, although these benefits come with increased weight and reduced breathability.

### Manufacturing Considerations

Using many smaller leather panels may also improve manufacturing efficiency.

Rather than requiring one large flawless section of leather, smaller pattern pieces can often be arranged around natural imperfections in the hide. This can increase material yield and reduce waste while maintaining a premium appearance.

### The Nike Swoosh

The stitched leather Swoosh serves more than a branding function.

Compared with a printed logo, the leather overlay:

* Creates depth and a three-dimensional appearance.
* Produces texture, shadows, and visual contrast.
* Contributes to the perception of craftsmanship and quality.
* Becomes an integrated structural element of the upper rather than a surface decoration.
* Improves durability by using a physical material instead of a printed graphic.

### Key Design Principle

Every panel, seam, and overlay represents a deliberate trade-off between:

* Performance
* Durability
* Manufacturing complexity
* Material cost
* Weight
* Aesthetics
* Brand identity

A successful footwear designer learns to understand **why** each feature exists before proposing improvements.

### Biggest Takeaway

The Air Jordan 1 is not simply a collection of leather pieces sewn together. Each panel, overlap, and construction decision appears to solve multiple design problems simultaneously while contributing to the shoe's iconic appearance.
